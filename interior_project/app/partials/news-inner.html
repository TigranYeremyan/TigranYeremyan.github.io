landscape wallpaper

Harlequin’s new collection of abstract and impressionistic non woven wallpapers, has captured the spirit of Scandinavian skies, to produce Landscapes, a modern- day collection.

The collection consists of 26 wallpapers in six designs, including artistic impressions of patchwork fields, mountain ranges, rolling hills and coastal seascapes, all of which are placed against a background of the horizon.This new collection’s patterns are printed in subtle shades, giving a sense of watery brushstrokes, while the colours play with light and shade. The collection uses soft diluted tones in combination with strong, earthy shades, such as sky and teal, ink and sea glass, smoke and charcoal, pebble and slate, grass and mustard, soft clover, mandarin, pomegranate and amethyst. Each design makes its own statement if used alone, but can be mixed together with other Landscapes patterns or colours to create your own personal work of art. To view this elegant collection,contact me for a viewing.
