designer anna cuthbert

I started my career as an Interior Designer 21 years ago and spent my childhood in the Interior Design industry through our family business. I am an Auckland based Interior Designer with a University degree behind me and Honours in Design from the renowned KLC School of Design in London. I have completed interior Design projects all over New Zealand and around the world.

I’m not your average interior designer, employing an understanding of the fundamentals of Interior Design I instinctively put together taste, style, pattern and colour to create for you a home which is a pleasure to live in. Good interior design is about creating real homes and spaces that work for those living or working within them and with elegance and timeless appeal.

A home is an extension of those within and that’s why I first spend time getting a greater appreciation of your needs, rather than just recommending the latest trends in the industry that will inevitably soon show their age. I can also take the stress out of your interior design project, managing your renovations, recommending and/or liaising with the other trades and professionals to ensure you get what you want and that you realise your vision.
